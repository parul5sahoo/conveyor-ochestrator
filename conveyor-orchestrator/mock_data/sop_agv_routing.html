SOP-2010: Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) Bypass & Bottleneck Mitigation

AGV Operational Protocols

In the event of a blocking condition, dispatch standard PickerBots to the affected aisle (e.g., Aisle 4) to collect inventory.

Task the dispatched AGV to "Route inventory to alternate conveyor CV-12". This bypass routing prevents physical pileups in the sorting lanes.
