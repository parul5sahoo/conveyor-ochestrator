SOP-4042: Conveyor CV-11 Calibration & Controller C-3 Reset

Mechanical Calibration Instructions

  1. Isolate power to Conveyor CV-11 main motor controller.
  2. Calibrate the main belt speed sensor to nominal operating limits (1.2m/s to 1.5m/s).
  3. Manually reset the C-3 digital controller by holding the physical reset switch for 5 seconds.
  4. Re-energize the drive system and execute a 10-second test rotation cycle.

Critical Safety & Compliance Protocols

  • Mechanical Path Lockout: Confirm a completely clear physical pathway around Conveyor CV-11 before calibrating sensors to prevent crush or pinch injuries.
  • Logistics Bypass: Ensure standard bypass mechanisms (such as Autonomous Guided Vehicle / AGV dispatches) are fully active to route pending load away from the blocked section.
  • PPE Requirement: Operational Safety limits require all technicians to wear standard high-visibility PPE, safety boots, and protective gloves.
